Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our laundry, cleaning, and landscaping services.

What are your service hours?

We operate from 8 AM to 8 PM, Monday through Saturday.

Do you offer pickup and delivery services?

Yes, we offer free pickup and delivery for laundry services within Lagos.

Do you provide cleaning services for offices?

Yes, we offer professional cleaning services for both residential and commercial spaces.

Can you handle large landscaping projects?

Absolutely! Our team is equipped to manage landscaping projects of all sizes, including garden design and maintenance.

How long does it take to process a typical laundry order?

Standard laundry orders are typically processed within 24-48 hours.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and major credit cards.

Orders can be canceled or changed up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time.
